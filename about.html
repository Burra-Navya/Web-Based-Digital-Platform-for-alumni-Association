About VBIT Alumni Association

Our Mission

To establish a strong institute by consistently maintaining state-of-the-art infrastructure and a cohesive, world-class team by providing need-based technical education.

Our Vision

To emerge as a premier institution for technical education in the country through academic excellence and to be recognized as a center for excellence in Research and Development.
